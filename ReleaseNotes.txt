	<h3>About the LTFlex</h3>
	<strong>Version 00.01.02</strong><br />
	<p>This is a fork of Chris Hammond's excellent HammerFlex skin built off of Bootstrap 3. LTFlex adds the following:
   <ul>
	  <li>Working Carousel with placehol.it images on Home.ascx.</li>
      <li>Inside.ascx skin page without the Carousel.</li>
	  <li>Changes the navbar to a fixed top style.</li>
	  <li>Adds some javascript to the Home.ascx page so that the fixed top navbar will not interfere with the admin control panel.</li>
	  <li>Modifies and adds several containers.</li>
   </ul>
   </p>	
   <p>Origninal HammerFlex Skin https://github.com/ChrisHammond/HammerFlex under Apache License v2.0</p>
